e-Law: an Iot Based Smart Home
Lighting System using WEMOS

The world's way of life is being significantly altered by automation. Users will be able to modify some parameters and track data regarding the operation of lighting accessories using a web browser connected to a private network by employing web development and Internet of Things technologies to design and construct a home lighting system. Numerous applications are created for the residents of the house to automatically control the lights' opening and closing, adjusting their brightness, and creating the scene. The smart lighting control system built on Arduino can provide us with the most comfortable ambient lighting through advanced management. It can reduce the energy consumption of the house by using energy in a way that is both most economical and practical. The industry will undoubtedly change as a result of the usage of smart lighting systems.
